Digital innovation in healthcare

Helping NHS organisations use digital technologies to improve life for patients, their informal carers and front-line clinicians.

About us

We started life in 2009 to help NHS organisations stay relevant in an emerging digital age.

Initially, we used our ‘fast focused innovation process’ with frontline NHS clinicians to create more than 20 prototypes between 2010 and 2015.

We then created and licensed Rally Round, an application that enables the family and friends of vulnerable people to create virtual support networks, so loved ones can have more independence at home.

Since 2018, we have focused entirely on Recap Health.

It’s an easy to use digital patient education platform, developed in close partnership with Nottinghamshire Healthcare NHS FT and University Hospitals of North Midlands NHS Trust.

Our team

At the heart of Health2Works is an experienced, knowledgeable and resilient team with a great digital technology skill set and a fundamental understanding of the NHS, its values and challenges.

The team consists of a combination of technical specialists, driven and intuitive directors and credible non executives, all with a proven track record of creating coherent web and mobile applications that benefit NHS patients, their families and front-line clinicians.
